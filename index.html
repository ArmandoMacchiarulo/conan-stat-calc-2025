Strength
Strength determines the damage you do with strength-based weapons and slightly increases how much you can carry and your damage with agility-based weapons.
Heavy Blows (5 points)
Heavy and special attacks deal roughly 10% more damage.
10-point perk (choose one)

Berserker (15 points)
While below 50% health you deal more damage and gain extra armor.
20-point perk (choose one)

Agility
Agility determines damage with agility-based weapons, improves your recovery after dodging and slightly increases stamina and strength-based damage.
Quickfooted (5 points)
Your dodges cost less stamina.
10-point perk (choose one)

Effortless Leap (15 points)
Jumping no longer drains stamina.
20-point perk (choose one)

Vitality
Vitality increases your health pool and how quickly you heal.
Healthy (5 points)
Increases health regeneration.
10-point perk (choose one)

Robust (15 points)
Increases your maximum health by a flat amount.
20-point perk (choose one)

Authority
Authority determines the damage your followers deal and increases your concussive damage for knocking out thralls.
Commanding Presence (5 points)
Followers in your presence gain bonus health / damage.
10-point perk (choose one)

Fervor (15 points)
Followers deal increased damage while near you.
20-point perk (choose one)

Grit
Grit increases your stamina pool and armor.
Tenacity (5 points)
Increases both armor and stamina by a fixed amount.
10-point perk (choose one)

Iron Hard Muscles (15 points)
Increases your natural armor.
20-point perk (choose one)

Expertise
Expertise increases the weight you can carry and how effective you are at harvesting resources.
Efficient Harvest (5 points)
You gather more resources from each node.
10-point perk (choose one)

Pack Mule (15 points)
Increases your carrying capacity significantly.
20-point perk (choose one)
